About LightBox

LightBox is your go-to tool for creating stunning and simple slideshows in minutes. Designed to be incredibly easy for everyone, from seniors sharing memories to professionals crafting presentations, LightBox helps you showcase your media beautifully.

Getting Started is Easy:

  1. Upload Your Media: Select images, videos (MP4, MOV, WebM, etc.), or audio files. Create a new "Lightbox" or add to an existing one.
  2. View Your Gallery: Manage your items in a clean gallery view. MKV files will show as "Processing" until converted to MP4.
  3. Launch the Slideshow: Click the play button to view your media in a smooth presentation.
  4. Manage Lightboxes: Rename, add more files, share, or delete your collections.

A Note on MKV File Uploads:

LightBox accepts MKV files! For smooth playback, MKVs are automatically converted to a highly compatible MP4 format (H.264 video, AAC audio) in the background. You'll see a "Processing..." status, and they become available once conversion is complete.

The typical FFmpeg command used is:

ffmpeg -i [input_mkv_file] -c:v libx264 -preset fast -crf 23 -c:a aac -b:a 128k -movflags +faststart -y [output_mp4_file]

Future: We're exploring options for different quality presets.
